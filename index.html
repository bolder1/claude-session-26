The arc

Two hours. Seven acts. From cold open to operating principles.

03
  1. 01

    The Cold Open

    No slides. A real miniOrange screen goes into Claude live: critique this, then rebuild it as an interactive React prototype. By the time intros are done, the artifact is rendering.

    5 min · live
  2. 02

    How Claude Actually Thinks

    Tokens, the context window as the intern's whiteboard, and why long chats get dumber. Interactive bit: lazy one-liner vs. proper brief, and you vote on which output came from which.

    15 min
  3. 03

    The Toolbox Tour

    Every surface, 30 seconds each: Chat, Artifacts, Projects, Skills, Connectors (MCP) into Figma & Notion, Plugins, Cowork, scheduled tasks & memory, and Claude Code for the curious.

    20 min
  4. 04

    The Precooked Showcase

    Real before/after pairs: 6-vendor research → 42 ranked requirements, PRD → 9-screen prototype, animated SVG decks, the --mo-* token spine, and a raw first output next to the shipped version.

    25 min
  5. 05

    Live Build — You Choose

    The room decides what gets built: a landing page we critique and iterate together, a rough sketch turned into a working prototype, or interview notes synthesized into themes and a persona.

    20 min · audience-driven
  6. 06

    Your First Week With Your Intern

    Hands-on. Everyone opens Claude on their own laptop, runs the starter prompt card written for their role, and a few of us share what came back. This is where it becomes yours.

    15 min · hands-on
  7. 07

    The Operating Principles

    The rule set you leave with: brief it like a junior, judge it like a director. New task, new chat. Context is currency. The first output is a sketch, never a comp.

    5 min
